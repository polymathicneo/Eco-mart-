Our Research

We looked at other websites to see what works well and what doesn't

Feature Amazon Other Eco Stores Our EcoMart Site
Design Lots of stuff everywhere, kinda cluttered Usually very minimal and plain Clean and modern but not boring
Navigation Complex menus with tons of categories Simple but sometimes too basic Easy to use with clear sections
Products Sells literally everything Only eco products but limited selection Focused on best sustainable items
User Experience Can be overwhelming with too many options Sometimes slow to load Fast and works well on phones

Websites We Looked At

Here are the sites we researched (at least 5 as required):

  1. Amazon - for general e-commerce design
  2. EcoStore - sustainable products store
  3. Organic Living - organic lifestyle products
  4. Green World - environmental organization site
  5. Sustainable Life - sustainability blog and shop

What We Learned

After checking out all these sites, we realized that many eco-friendly stores look too plain and boring. We wanted EcoMart to look professional and modern while still being easy to navigate. We also made sure our site loads quickly because nobody wants to wait forever for a page to load.

We picked green colors obviously since it fits with the environmental theme, but we didn't overdo it. The layout uses CSS Grid which we learned about in class - it automatically adjusts for different screen sizes which is really cool.
