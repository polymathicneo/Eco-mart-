Design References

Our design system was inspired by nature and modern web trends.

  • Color Palette: Forest Green (#2E7D32) represents nature/growth, Earth Tone (#8D6E63) represents stability/soil, and Gold (#FBC02D) adds a touch of premium warmth.
  • Typography: 'Montserrat' for headers provides a strong, modern structure, while 'Lato' ensures excellent readability for body text.
  • Layout: We utilized CSS Grid and Flexbox to create a fully responsive layout that adapts seamlessly to all device sizes.
